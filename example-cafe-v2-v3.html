Our Story
A Café Worth
Coming Back To

We opened in 2014 with one goal — to be the café we always wanted but could never find. No pretension, no overpriced drinks in tiny cups. Just great food, good coffee, and a space where you can actually relax.

We're proudly dog-friendly, have got plug sockets if you need to work, and we've been part of the Wimborne community for over a decade.
