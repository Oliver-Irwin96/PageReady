What We Offer

Real Ales

Six rotating cask ales, always including at least two local Dorset breweries. Ask what's on.

Home-Cooked Food

Traditional pub classics made fresh every day. Sunday roast available every week — booking advised.

Open Fire

Log fires in the bar all winter. The best seat in Wimborne on a cold evening, guaranteed.

Dog Friendly

Dogs welcome in the bar. Water bowls always on, dog biscuits behind the bar.

Over 130 Years
of Good Company

The Crown & Anchor has been at the heart of Wimborne since 1887. Five landlords, two world wars, and countless lock-ins later — we're still here, and we wouldn't have it any other way.

We're not a chain, not a gastro pub, and not trying to be anything we're not. Just a proper local, run by people who love their community.
