A little about us
Small Enough to Know Your Name

The Harbour Rooms has been welcoming guests since 2009 — a family-run B&B perched above Swanage Bay with six individually decorated rooms, each with a sea or garden view.

We're not a hotel. We're somewhere to actually relax. You'll get a proper breakfast, a recommendation for a walk that isn't in any guidebook, and someone who genuinely wants you to have a good time.
