About Us
Qualified, Experienced, Local

We're a small, independent physio clinic based in Wimborne — treating patients from across Dorset. No waiting lists, no referrals, and no one-size-fits-all treatment plans.

Every assessment is thorough, every treatment plan is tailored to you. We want to understand what's causing your pain and fix it properly, not just manage the symptoms.
