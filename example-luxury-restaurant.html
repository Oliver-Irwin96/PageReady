Our Philosophy

Cooking With
Intention

Ember & Stone opened in 2018 with a single idea: that the best dining experiences come from restraint. From knowing what to leave out as much as what to put in.

Every dish is built around the open fire — a wood-burning hearth that drives our menu from the first course to the last. Suppliers are chosen because we know their names, not just their postcodes.
