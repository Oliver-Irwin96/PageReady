Our Story
Six Decades of
Fine Craft

Harrington & Co. was founded in 1962 by Gerald Harrington, a master goldsmith trained in London's Hatton Garden. Today his granddaughter Claire continues the tradition in the same Bournemouth studio.

We have never been a chain. Every piece passes through the same hands, with the same attention to detail that has kept customers returning — and recommending us — for generations.
